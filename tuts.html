How to Save SHSH2 Blobs for iOS

This is a Tutorial on how to save SHSH2 Blobs for iOS, most of you will have no idea what that means so i'll tell you. Apple has 2 Signing services, 1 of them is signing certificates for apps, the second signing service is for iOS Versions. The one we will focus on is the iOS version one, So if a iOS version is signed you can just downgrade with iTunes, but if its unsigned you can't downgrade, But that's were SHSH2 Blobs come in, SHSH2 Blobs kind of save the signing hash so you can downgrade, (a great way to check whats signed and unsigned by going to ipsw.me and selecting your device then it will show all iOS versions).
So lets get into the Tutorial:
Step 1: Plug in your iOS Device to your computer using a lighting to USB cord.
Step 2: Open up iTunes (if you haven't got it download it from apples website)
Step 3: Go to This Website
Step 4: Go back to iTunes and click on the little device icon and click on serial number twice and it will change to ECID copy that and put it into tsssaver.
Step 5: Then select your device model and verify that you're not a Robot and it will bring you to a site that has some info (may take a while to load) Then just click the orange link and you have your blobs.
(I recommend bookmarking the page) If you want to restore with your blobs go to youtube and type "How to use Future Restore".
